Imprint & Privacy Policy

Imprint

This website is operated exclusively for family and private purposes.
It is a private project without any commercial intentions, designed to organize internal family processes.

Privacy Information

We take the protection of your data seriously, even within our small family circle. Here is a brief overview of what happens when you use this app:

1. What data is collected?

To ensure the features of the app (e.g., voting on names or reserving flea market items) function properly, we store the following information locally on our own server:

  • Entered Names: The names you enter when making suggestions, in the flea market ("I'll get it") or as comments.
  • IP Addresses (temporary): To prevent someone (or a bot) from voting 100 times, and to protect against overload ("Rate Limiting"), we store your device's IP address anonymously or temporarily at the time of voting.

2. Hosting and Service Providers

The app is hosted completely on our private home server. Your name suggestions and comments do not leave our home. There is no evaluation by third parties (like Google Analytics, Facebook, etc.).

To make the site securely accessible from the internet, we use the Cloudflare service. This routes the data traffic (like a kind of protective shield) to our server and stores technically necessary connection data (e.g., IP addresses) in accordance with its own privacy policy.

3. Cookies

We do not use any tracking or advertising cookies. The app only stores technically strictly necessary data in the so-called "Local Storage" of your browser (e.g., whether you have activated the admin mode) to guarantee the functionality of the site.

4. Deletion of Data

As soon as our baby is here and the app has fulfilled its purpose, the entire server will be shut down and all associated data will be completely deleted.
